Next Syllable Prediction

This demo showcases a machine learning model that predicts the next syllable as you type. It's designed to assist with writing in languages where syllable structure is important.

How to Use

Simply start typing in the text box below. The model will provide a "ghost" suggestion for the next syllable directly in the text box, and three alternative suggestions will appear below. You can click on any of the suggestions to append it to your text.
